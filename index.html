Design Process

From Concept to Creation

We believe in a collaborative and iterative approach to web design and development. Through our process, we work with you to develop a strategy and create a design that reflects your brand and meets your needs.

1. Discovery

  • Research industry & competitors
  • Define customers and target market
  • Define overall website goals

2. Planning

  • Develop SEO Strategy
  • Create sitemap and content copy
  • Clarify objective for design

3. Design

  • Wireframes: form and functionality
  • Colors, Typography and Imagery
  • Finalize overall look and designs

4. Development

  • Technical configuration and set up
  • Implement designs and requirements
  • Implement SEO best practices

5. Analytics

  • Full website review: Q&A
  • Implement Tools: Analytics & Tracking
  • Promote on social media channels

6. Maintenance

  • Set up advertising campaign
  • Review performance, metrics & ranking
  • A/B testing: changes for improvement
